Detailed Description

Controls frisbee accumulation.

This is the part of the robot responsible for picking up Frisbees and getting them into the robot. In its most basic form, it consists of one motor that will run a series of rollers and belts that will intake the discs. Depending on feasibility, this will also feature an arm to flip upside down Frisbees before they go into the robot.
